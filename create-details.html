Step-by-Step Guide to Recreate Outfits Using Thrifted Items

Step 1: Select and Purchase Thrifted Items

  1. Choose Items: Select a variety of thrifted clothing items that you find interesting and versatile.
  2. Purchase: Add the selected items to your cart and complete the purchase.

Step 2: Plan Your Outfits

  1. Mix and Match: Think about how different items can be paired together. Consider colors, patterns, and styles.
  2. Create Looks: Plan a few outfits by laying out the clothes and imagining different combinations. Take photos for reference if needed.
  3. Accessorize: Think about accessories that can enhance each outfit, such as belts, scarves, jewellery, hats, and shoes.

Step 3: Style Your Outfits

  1. Layering: Use layering to add depth and interest to your outfits. For example, layer a thrifted denim jacket over a dress or pair a turtleneck under a sleeveless top.
  2. Contrasting Styles: Combine different styles for a unique look. Pair a vintage skirt with a modern top or mix a formal blazer with casual jeans.
  3. Color Coordination: Pay attention to color coordination. Use complementary or contrasting colors to create visually appealing outfits.
  4. Pattern Mixing: Experiment with mixing patterns. Pair a striped top with a floral item.

Step 4: Photograph Your Outfits

  1. Take High-Quality Photos: Find a well-lit area and set up a simple backdrop if possible. Capture each outfit from multiple angles to showcase the entire look.
  2. Edit Photos: Use photo-editing software to enhance the images if needed.

Step 5: Share and Submit Your Outfits

  1. Upload to Myntra: Go to the Thrift and Create section on Myntra and upload your photos.
  2. Engage with Community: Share your recreated looks on social media, tag Myntra, using #thriftwithmyntra.
