Buying Thrifted Items

Step 1: Browse Thrift Section
Go to the Thrift and Create section dedicated to pre-loved items.
Step 2: Search for Items
Scroll through the listings to find items that interest you. Click on items to view more details.
Step 3: Evaluate Items
  1. Read Descriptions: Carefully read the item descriptions provided by the seller, including the condition, size, and any flaws or unique features.
  2. Check Photos: Look at all the photos to get a clear idea of the item's condition and appearance.
Step 4: Make a Purchase
  1. Add to Cart: Once you find an item you like, add it to your cart.
  2. Checkout: Proceed to checkout, review your order, and make the payment using your preferred payment method.

Selling Thrifted Items

Step 1: Prepare Your Items
  1. Select Items: Choose items from your wardrobe that you want to sell. Ensure they are in good condition.
  2. Clean and Repair: Wash, iron, and repair any minor flaws to make your items more appealing to buyers.
Step 2: Create a Listing
  1. Open Myntra App: Launch the Myntra app and go to the Thrift and Create section.
  2. List an Item: Click on the option to list a new item for sale.
Step 3: Provide Item Details
  1. Title: Write a clear, descriptive title for your item (e.g., "Vintage Denim Jacket").
  2. Description: Provide a detailed description, including size, brand, condition, and any unique features.
  3. Photos: Upload high-quality photos of the item from different angles. Ensure good lighting and a clean background.
Step 4: Manage Listings
  1. Review Listings: Check your listings to ensure all details are accurate.
  2. Respond to Inquiries: Promptly respond to any questions from potential buyers through the Myntra app.
Step 5: Complete the Sale
  1. Packaging: Package the item securely to prevent damage during transit. Use eco-friendly packaging if possible.
  2. Ship Item: Your item will be collected at your door by our Myntra delivery partners.
